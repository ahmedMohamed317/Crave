Crave App:
Used Sdk version is 29 and android studio dolphin version 

This application is an application for meal recipes and meals planning 
You can signup , login using Github or Google and even you can use the Guest mode 
There are animation lotties and scrollabe views on the starting screens 
In home screen you can find the Dish of the day meal , and list of Suggested dishes 
you can search for a meal using countries , categories or even ingredients 
you can view the meal and see a pic of it , the procedures , the ingredients and even a youtube video illustrating the procedures 
you can use the favourite section to save your favourite meals
In the Calender Section you can plan for the day you want to have your meal
